31 August 2021

MarkSuckerberg updated:

  • IPCs no longer have useless lungs.
  • IPC brains can now be removed as posibrains and then any posibrain can be stuck into an IPC body. Like it was supposed to a year ago.
  • You can actually revive IPCs by healing them above 0 health.
  • You can actually do surgery steps that require an empty hand on carbons again.
  • aheals no longer ghost you
  • IPCs actually have oil blood now. Probably.
  • You can now set mutantorgans to `null` to just not give the species an organ there.

  • MMI examine text now accounts for the fact that IPCs do not have human lungs inside them anymore
